# What is EMDR & How does it work?
- **EMDR**--Eye Movement Desensitization & Reprocessing
    - Releasing the emotional charge associated with a distressing event through left-right eye movement (a movement that emulated REM sleep)
    - The eye movement back and fourth created an opening in the nervous system and started to regulate the nervous system -> **theory** opened a connection between the left and right sides of the brain
        - When traumatic event happen, the thinking part of the brain shuts down -> idea is to open up the thinking part of the brain whilst "re-experiencing" the traumatic event
- **Goal**
    - Support a safe and relaxed state to process a traumatic memory
