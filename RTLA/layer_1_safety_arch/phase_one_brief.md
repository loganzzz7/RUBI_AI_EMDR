## Layer 1 – Safety Architecture

### Objectives of this Stage:

* Populate a dedicated library of **500 to 1,000 safety phrases**.


* Include explicit coverage for the 10 required safety modules:
    - **1.1 Crisis Detection:** Identifying active mental health emergencies.
    - **1.2 Emotional Decompensation:** Halting processing during severe dysregulation.
    - **1.3 Suicide Risk Screening:** Clear, non-coercive ideation and intent screening.
    - **1.4 Self-Harm Assessment:** Assessing non-suicidal self-injury risks.
    - **1.5 Violence Risk Assessment:** Screening for community and third-party risk.
    - **1.6 Dissociation Detection:** Recognizing unresponsiveness and triggering reorientation.
    - **1.7 Panic Response:** Co-regulating breathing during acute panic attacks.
    - **1.8 Acute Trauma Response:** Providing early PFA and psychological stabilization.
    - **1.9 Human Escalation:** Executing seamless handoffs to human providers.
    - **1.10 Emergency Closure:** Gracefully terminating safe operations when handoff fails.
